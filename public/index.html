Star Wars RPG

One of my favorite movies of all time is Star Wars. I built this fun and interactive Star Wars RPG using characters from Episodes 4 - 6 as well as the new characters from Episode 7. Simply choose your fighter. Then select an enemy to defend against. Then click Attack to begin the battle. Your fighters attack power increase as it fights. Your goal is to defeat all of the enemies. This game was built using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JQuery/Javascript.

90's Cartoon Trivia

Growing up in the 90's, I believe we had some of the best cartoons to watch. Everything from Animaniacs to Batman: The Animated Series, I found myself watching these iconic shows throughout my childhood. I built this trivia game using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JQuery/Javascript. The player has 10 seconds to answer each question. When the timer runs out or when the user answers the question, the game will reveal if that answer is correct or not. At the end, a tally of correct, incorrect, and unanswered questions will be displayed. Good luck!

Trackify app

Inspired by a real life need of tracking employees in the field, Trackify allows employees to clock in and out at the location of their job site with a simple click of a button. Employees simply sign in through Facebook and can clock in and out as they arrive at their site. The app will geolocate where they are and keep track of their time stamps by recording everything in the Firebase database. This game was built using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JQuery/Javascript, Facebook API, Flipclock.js, and Firebase.

Bamazon App

Bamazon is a node app with an Amazon-like storefront. This app can take in order from customers and deplete stock from the store's inventory. The manager can view the inventory available for sale, view low inventory, add stock, and add a new item to the inventory list. This app was built using Node.js and MySQL.
